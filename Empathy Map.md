Summary: Builds empathy by capturing explicit and implicit experiences to reveal emotional tensions and motivations.
Purpose: Reveal user emotions, thoughts, actions, and tensions for deeper understanding.
Design Phase:
  - "[[Notion/Design Thinking Agent/Design Thinking Stages/Empathize|Empathize]]"
Pitfalls: Taking guesses instead of observed data; overgeneralizing; skipping synthesis.
Prompts: |-
  • “What does the user feel but not say?“
  • “What contradictions exist between thoughts and actions?“
  • “What surprised you about their context?”
Outputs / Artifacts: Empathy Map, insight clusters, user quotes.
Use Case:
  - Early Discovery
  - User Interviews
  - Emotional Mapping
  - New Audience Understanding
  - Pain Point Discovery
Best For: Early discovery, personas, onboarding research, problem reframing.
Teams & Stakeholders: Researchers, product designers, PMs, content strategists.
Steps / Structure: |-
  • Steps:
      1. Collect note
      2. Fill four quadrants
      3. Highlight tensions 
      4. Synthesize insights.
  • Quadrants: Says / Thinks / Does / Feels
  • Use: Capture user insights and emotional states.
