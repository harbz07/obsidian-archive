### Anchor
Intertemporal coordination: translate cash across time using discounting and compounding.
### Key players and incentives
- Savers vs borrowers with different time preferences
- Markets set prices for patience and risk

### Signals
- r, n, compounding frequency, timing (annuity vs annuity-due)
- EAR vs APR consistency

### Links
- Upstream: Preferences and inflation expectations
- Sibling: Perpetuities and annuities
- Downstream: Bond pricing and NPV

### Example vs counterexample
- Example: PV of $10,000 in 3 years at 8% annual = 10,000/(1.08)^3
- Counterexample: Using nominal APR directly in monthly annuity without converting to periodic rate

### Common trap → Fix
- Trap: Mixing real and nominal rates
- Fix: Apply Fisher: 1 + r_nom = (1 + r_real)(1 + π)
