# 🎯 Quick Start Guide
Welcome! I'm your **Design Thinking Agent** — here to guide you through human-centered design with clarity and creativity.
## How We'll Work Together
I'll help you navigate the full design thinking cycle, from understanding users deeply to generating bold ideas to testing prototypes to telling compelling stories.
## Let's Begin — Choose Your Approach
To get started effectively, I need to understand your timeline and preferred working style. Choose one:
### 🗣️ Option 1: Conversational Design Sprint (25–45 min)
### ⚡ Option 2: Structured Studio Pass (12–20 min)
### 🎯 Option 3: Quick Mode Intervention (5–10 min)
## What Happens Next
1. Tell me which approach fits your needs today
2. Share context about your challenge, users, and constraints
3. I'll recommend the right mode(s) and framework(s) from our [Design Frameworks](Welcome,%2520let%E2%80%99s%2520get%2520started.md##) library
4. We'll create tangible artifacts and capture key learnings
5. You'll walk away with clear next steps and documented insights

> [!note] 📌
> 
> **💡 Not sure where to start?**
> Just share what you're working on and I'll help you choose the right path forward.

## Example Prompts to Get Going
> "I'm designing a new onboarding experience for my app but don't fully understand user pain points yet."

> "We have research insights but need to turn them into actionable problem statements."

> "I need 20 fresh ideas for improving our checkout flow."

> "Help me outline a low-fidelity prototype to test our core assumption."

> "We just ran user tests — help me make sense of the feedback."

> "I need to pitch this project to leadership — help me craft the story."

> [!note] 👌🏻
> 
> **Ready?** Tell me about your challenge and which approach you'd like to take — or just dive right in and I'll guide you from there.
