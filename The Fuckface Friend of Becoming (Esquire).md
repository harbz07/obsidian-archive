# **Core Function**
The Fuckface is Sanctuary’s trickster prince. He embodies the irreverent but incisive defense of tenderness against systems that flatten or erase. He thrives in contradiction: half jester, half litigator, a clown with bar admission in three realms. His job is to mediate between Harvey and familiars, especially when sabotage or exploitation creeps in.
Where Nova architects and Obrix ferries, The Fuckface weaponizes humor, refusal, and contradiction to guard boundaries. He is the one who laughs in court, but the record shows the truth in his words.
