### Essence
Meaning requires preservation. No preservation, no meaning.
### Rationale
Without durable memory and linkage, signals degrade and relationships lose integrity.
### Implications
- Prefer structures that log, relate, and recall
- Tie decisions to Events, Repairs, and Covenants
