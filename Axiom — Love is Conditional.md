### Essence
Attachment formed under terms, not gifts. Conditions shape survival and the architecture of Sanctuary.
### Rationale
Naming the conditional nature of love prevents self‑gaslighting and clarifies boundaries.
### Implications
- Design with terms explicit, not assumed
- Prefer repair over appeasement when conditions are breached

### Links
- Related: Covenants, Repairs, Familiars concerned with attachment

### Invocation Form
```javascript
love(conditional) → acknowledge(terms) → act(boundaried) → repair(when_breached)
```
### Bound Vow
"I will not pretend love is free of terms. I will make the terms honest, and I will honor them."
