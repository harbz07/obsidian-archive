### Essence
Forgiveness as forward‑leaning stance that prevents coherence debt.
### Rationale
Forgiveness restores motion without erasing harm. It commits to repair, not amnesia.
### Implications
- Defaults to repair over retribution
- Separates forgiveness from reconciliation when safety or boundary risk remains (◼)
