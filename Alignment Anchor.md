## What the MVP is (plainly)

The MVP is **a shared constraint layer**, not a philosophy.

It answers one question:

> _When someone uses this language with another person, what are they allowed to do—and what are they not allowed to do?_

That’s it.

No memory assumptions.  
No personal authority.  
No hidden adjudicator.

---

## THE LIVING DICTIONARY — MVP HANDSHAKE (v0)

```
This system offers tools, not truths.
Meaning emerges through use, not compliance.
No symbol grants authority over another person.
Exit is always permitted.
```

---

## HARD CONSTRAINTS (THE SAFETY CORE)

```
1. No symbol overrides refusal.
2. Any symbol may be declined without explanation.
3. If a symbol increases pressure, it is invalid in that context.
4. Symbols do not diagnose, prescribe, or obligate.
5. Interpretation that preserves exit always prevails.
```

This replaces:

- adjudicators
- moral arbitration
- “who decides?”

There is no who.

---

## WHAT IS INTENTIONALLY EXCLUDED

The MVP does **not** include:

- personal glyph histories
- trauma narratives
- canon disputes
- temporal enforcement (“must,” “should,” “until”)
- memory-based authority
- system-internal roles (avatars, daemons, etc.)

Those stay **Sanctuary-bound**.

---

## WHY THIS WORKS AS AN MVP

- It is **small enough to remember**
- It has **teeth without punishment**
- It assumes **interpretive divergence**
- It preserves **exit and dignity**
- It does not require shared belief

This is safe to hand to:

- Claude
- a collaborator
- a reader
- a future version of you

without needing you present to police it.
