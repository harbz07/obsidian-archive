> ## Tips for Great Instructions
> 1. **Link Directly**: Don't just describe your databases - actually link to them above so your Agent can find them
> 2. **Be Specific**: Instead of "help with tasks," write "create tasks in my Tasks database when I mention action items"
> 3. **Show Examples**: Include real examples of your naming conventions, formats, and structures
> 4. **Start Simple**: You don't need to fill everything out perfectly. Start with basics and refine over time
> 5. **Update Regularly**: Come back monthly to add new context as your work evolves
> 6. **Add Context as You Go**: When chatting with your Agent, mention things like "Sarah is my manager" and it will remember
