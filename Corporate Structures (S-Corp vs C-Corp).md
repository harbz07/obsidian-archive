### Anchor
Entity choice trades off control, liability, and taxation to align incentives among capital providers and operators.
### Key players and incentives
- Founders and managers: control, liability protection
- Investors: claim priority, governance rights, tax pass-through
- State/IRS: compliance and tax base

### Signals
- C-Corp double taxation as costly signal of scale and permanence
- LP/LLC structures signal flexibility and pass-through priorities

### Links
- Upstream: Agency problems and governance
- Sibling: Dividend policy as signal
- Downstream: Cost of capital and capital structure

### Example vs counterexample
- Example: Venture-backed startup chooses C-Corp to issue preferred equity
- Counterexample: Lifestyle business stays LLC to keep pass-through taxation

### Common trap → Fix
- Trap: Assuming S-Corp avoids payroll taxes on all payouts
- Fix: Separate reasonable salary from distributions per rules
