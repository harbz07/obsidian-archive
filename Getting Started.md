This is your step-by-step guide to make the most of your workspace.
Follow the flow to move smoothly from raw ideas to actionable projects and tasks.
## **✅ Quick guide to use**

---
### **Capture all your unfiltered ideas with the **<u>**Quick Buttons**</u>
### **Review and classify your ideas in **<u>**Creative Dumping**</u>**.**
### **Send promising ideas to the**** **<u>**Idea Vault**</u>**.**
### **Turn the best ideas into mini-projects with the **<u>**Transformer**</u>**.**
### **Break projects down into actionable tasks in the **<u>**Project Panel.**</u>
### **Keep an eye on your future ideas in the **<u>**Inspiration Library**</u>**.**

Your system is a living ecosystem.
- Keep your Creative Dumping active. The more you capture, the more opportunities you’ll discover.
- Regularly revisit your vault to spot ideas that are ready for action.
- Archive ideas that no longer align with your direction — this clears mental space.

### 🚀 Result: From scattered ideas to focused execution.
By following this flow, you’ll build a natural rhythm:
- You’ll never lose a good idea.
- You’ll stop overthinking when to act.
- And you’ll always know what your next step is.

## Internal navigation index

---
[📥 Creative Dumping](/24298e9c907e802b8db3d8d59500ed7a)
This home-section is your chaotic inbox. Everything that comes to your mind unfiltered: loose ideas, thoughts, mental scribbles.
[🌱 Idea Vault](/24298e9c907e81eebf90d16a483f5764)
This is where you take the valuable Creative Dumping entries, already cleaned and refined. It is the ordered and structured database of ideas that have potential.
[⚡ Project Panel](/24298e9c907e81f1a267c390bc6c6fc7)
When an idea is validated or you see that it deserves action, it becomes a project in this section. Here you define tasks, objectives, timelines, etc.
[🗂️ Inspiration Library](/24298e9c907e813c9321d76b5b21ece1)
Here we collect resources, references, snippets, images, links, etc. They are not business ideas as such, but they feed your creativity and can inspire new entries in the Creative Dumping or enrich existing ideas in the Idea Vault.
[🗑️ Archive](/24298e9c907e814ab9affd6fe3dbbe9b)
Even archived ideas have value. The "Archive" view lets you revisit them for future inspiration.
