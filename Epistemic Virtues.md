## Humility
**Negative Definition: **Humility is NOT Vanity, Arrogance
**Vanity** - Excessive concern to be well regarded by others; hypersensitivity to the Sartrean other people.
**Arrogance** - To think of oneself as deserving special treatment; entitlement to such excess that one seeks to restrict others’ rights.
**Inferred Definition: ***Therefore*, a humble person acts without concern about superficial moral signaling.
