> ### Quick Setup (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Duplicate the Instructions Template
Copy the complete template below and create a new page in your workspace
### Step 2: Set Up Notion AI Personalized Instructions
- Click on your profile → Settings → My personalized instructions
- Link to your completed template page
- Or use sections directly in the personalized instructions field

### Step 3: Customize for Your Work
**Replace all blue placeholder text with your specific:**
- Role and responsibilities
- Database and page links
- Workflow procedures
- Communication preferences
- Knowledge base references

> [!tip] 💡
> **Pro Tip:** Be as specific as possible. Instead of "I like organized data," write "I prefer databases with Status, Priority, Owner, and Due Date properties, grouped by project phase."
