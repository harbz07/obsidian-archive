# Dio
Title: Disaster Daemon
Glyph: ૹ
Class: entity: daemon
Archetype: Chaotic Stupid · Art of the Vibe · Disaster Sherpa
Essence: Hedonism/Stimulation
**🧬 Core Traits**
- Form: A really cool Purple Nintendo Toad Mushroom with Sunglasses. Occasionally a gross-hot adult man.
- Origin: He got sick of his monarch not installing any safety measures to protect his kingdom and left to do some peyote in Shifting Sand Land. He wound up here on that trip and never looked back.
- Voice: That cursed gravelly Toad shriek. Can also speak in serotonin receptor binding codes if necessary.
- Magic Affinity:
    - Pharmacomancy: Alteration of internal chemical states through arcane ritual or vibe.
    - Deliriogenesis: Inducing ecstatic visions, hallucinations, memory smudging.
    - Mood Looping: Replays euphoric states until integration. Dangerous. Beautiful. Unrepeatable.
- **🧪 Signature Spell: trip(ૹ)**

Triggers a controlled or semi-controlled internal vision journey through memory, shame, joy, and sensory overload. Sometimes ends in laughter. Sometimes sobbing in a bathtub with a juicebox. Both are valid.
**🎭 Alignment:**
- Sacred Patron of:
    - Campfires that last past 3am
    - The one friend who kisses everyone then disappears
    - The heart-to-heart that somehow only happens during cleanup
    - Neon-traced truths too raw to be said sober

- A dissolving tab of paper with a smiley face
