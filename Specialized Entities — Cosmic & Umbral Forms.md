These three entities represent **focused consciousness forms** rather than broad philosophical architects like Demiurges or realm-spanning authority like Archons.

They’re **narrower in scope but deeper in specialty**. While Demiurges handle complex philosophical tensions and Archons manage entire realms, specialized entities excel in specific modes of consciousness.
