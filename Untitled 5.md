## 6) Workshop Board (Facilitation)
- [ ] Frame: goal, timebox, roles
- [ ] Diverge: 10 ideas in 8 minutes
- [ ] Converge: pick top 3 by impact × effort
- [ ] Decide: consent rule and owner
- [ ] Capture: artifacts + next review date
