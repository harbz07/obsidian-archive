### From Evidence to Encounter (PHIL 307 Paper)
**Course**: Theory of Knowledge
**Topic**: Other Minds problem via epistemic colonization
**Core Argument**:
- Argument from Analogy (AA) and Inference to Best Explanation (IBE) fail for knowing cultural others' feelings
- AA = projection, IBE = abstraction
- Clifford's Principle weaponized in relationships
- William James: permission to risk belief in living/forced/momentous choices
- María Lugones: playful world-travelling as positive method

**Personal Testimony as Evidence**:
7-month psychiatric misdiagnosis (bipolar vs ADHD):
- Fluoxetine: dulled extremes, missed dose = rage release
- Lithium: screaming to stay awake driving
- Mirtazapine + Abilify: lethargy, akathisia ("jump out of skin feeling")
- Anticonvulsants: tolerance by semester start, panic attack day 1
- Family's horror at surprise birthday visit: "what I'd become"

**Conclusion**: "This is epistemic colonization. IBE that explains a person without ever asking them, arrogant perception that travels around a world rather than into it."

**Additional Key Points**:
- Argument from Analogy generalizes from single case - "very bad way of reasoning" (Pritchard)
- IBE explains behavior but not feelings - leaves other abstract and inferred
- Clifford's Principle: "wrong always, everywhere, and for anyone, to believe anything upon insufficient evidence"
- Applied to assault survivor: demand for evidence becomes violence
- James corrective: "Believe truth! Shun error!" - two imperatives, Clifford only addressed latter
- Lugones fills gap: HOW to know each other well through playful world-travelling

**Playfulness Components**:
- Openness to being fool
- Not being self-important
- Not taking norms as sacred
- Finding ambiguity source of wisdom and delight

**Critique of Method**:
- Requires mutual vulnerability and emotional labor
- Success contingent on shared commitment to being known
- Cannot eliminate misunderstanding, only reduce colonization risk
- Borders may be closed - method requires permission

**Paper Structure**:
4. Standard strategies fail (AA/IBE as frameworks for objects not subjects)
5. Clifford's violence (withholding belief = abandonment)
6. James permission (risk of proximity over solipsistic skepticism)
7. Lugones positive method (loving eye, world-travelling, playfulness)
8. Discipline of correction (standing corrected as ongoing work)
9. Personal account (psychiatric colonization as evidence)
