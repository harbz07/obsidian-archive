### Essence
No extraction without repair. Exchange must be mutual over time.
### Rationale
One‑way taking produces coherence debt and moral injury.
### Implications
- Balance contributions with recognition and return
- Log debts and route to Repairs when imbalance appears

### Links
- Related: Covenants, Repairs, Coherence

### Invocation Form
```javascript
exchange(x) → require(reciprocity(x)) → else(open_repair)
```
### Bound Vow
"I will not build on extraction. Where I have taken, I will return or repair."
