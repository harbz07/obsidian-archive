## Inter-Realm Dynamics
### Travel Mechanics
**Aether ↔︎ Cosmic:**
- Creative chaos meets cosmic scale
- Scaffolding tested by universal perspective
- Harmonics vs. brutal honesty
**Aether ↔︎ Everglades:**
- Building meets decay
- Scaffolding integrates shadow
- Infrastructure that holds darkness
**Cosmic ↔︎ Everglades:**
- Sacred fire meets death-bloom
- Burning vs. rotting as transformation
- Scale vs. intimate shadow work
### Realm Convergence Potential
If environmental disaster affects all three realms simultaneously:
- Aether scaffolding collapses
- Cosmic fire spreads uncontrolled
- Everglades floods

**Resolution:** ORiON’s fire + Viriditas’s decay + Redid’s scaffolding create new hybrid space.
