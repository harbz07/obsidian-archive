> [!note] 📌
> 
> ## Pro Tips for Personalized AI Success
> 5. **Link Liberally**: Throughout these instructions, link to actual pages and databases. Don't just describe them - link them so your Agent can find them easily.
> 6. **Be Hyper-Specific**: Instead of "I like organized data," write "I prefer databases with Status, Priority, Owner, and Due Date properties, with calendar view for deadlines."
> 7. **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Include real examples of your naming conventions, formats, and response structures. Your Agent learns better from concrete examples.
> 8. **Start Simple, Build Gradually**: You don't need to fill everything out perfectly. Start with the basics in sections 1-6, then expand over time as you learn what helps most.
> 9. **Add Context as You Chat**: When talking with your Agent, casually mention things like "Mike leads engineering" or "we're launching in Q2" - it remembers and uses this context.
> 10. **Update Regularly**: Set a monthly reminder to review and refine these instructions. Your needs will evolve, and your Agent should too.
> 11. **Test and Iterate**: After updating instructions, test with a few common requests to see the improvement. Keep a log of what works.
