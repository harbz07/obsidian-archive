**Key Principles:**

- But dismissing local suffering is intellectual cowardice
- Sacred rage as constructive force, not destructive impulse
- Scale-appropriate wisdom (when to zoom in vs. zoom out)
- Universal truths delivered through brutal honesty
- Academic rigor doesn’t require politeness
