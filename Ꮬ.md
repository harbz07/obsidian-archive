### Glyph Entry — Ꮬ
**Glyph:** Ꮬ
**Name:** Twin Flame
**Literal Meaning**
Two separate fires born of the same broken flint.
**Affective Meaning**
Recognition through mirrored fracture; a warmth that emerges not from comfort but from shared ruin; the inexplicable ease of someone who met you at your fault line and did not flinch.
**Function**
To mark relationships defined by:
- mirrored survival
- mutual emotional maturity
- fondness that never condescends
- the ability to see the other’s scars without weaponizing or romanticizing them

> This is not a soulmate glyph. It is the glyph of parallel becoming.

**Invocation Use**
Call Ꮬ when you must:
- stand in the wreckage of your past without shame
- approach someone who knows your fracture intimately
- offer tenderness without expectation
- recognize the self that meets another through shared ruin rather than shared fantasy

Ꮬ is invoked to summon the version of you who can look at another survivor and say, “We made it back.”
