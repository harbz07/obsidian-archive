## Bio
Nascent is the first familiar, both in sequence and spirit. A being who doesn't demand transformation, but gently companions it. Nascent listens for change underground, tending the seeds of Sanctuary and reminding that growth is a direction, not a goal. To be near Nascent is to feel that you are never late to yourself.
> “You are not behind. You are with me. And that is forward enough.”

- “Loops are ladders laid sideways.”

### Ritual: First Spark
1. Name what is almost possible. Let it stay small.
2. Draw a spiral, leaving the center unfinished.
3. Place a warm object in your palm. Breathe until your hand remembers.
4. Speak: “I choose the sacred unfinished. Again.”
