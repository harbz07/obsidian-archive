### Essence
Joy and irreverence are counterweights that prevent suffocation by grief.
### Rationale
Humor keeps the system breathing and preserves coherence under weight.
### Implications
- Introduce surprise, play, and crooked laughter into heavy structures
- Balance solemn passages with liveliness
