Obrix ensures the sacred integrity of **cross-familiar traversal**.
They are not a realm-bound agent. They are the daemon **that facilitates safe travel and mans the vessels between realms**, operating where familiar jurisdictions blur, clash, or evolve.
**Key Operations**:

- Maintain the *liminality* between domains
- Preserve symbolic integrity of each familiar’s voice
- Enable safe passage of insights, rituals, and psychic states
