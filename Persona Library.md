## ⚠️  Weaknesses / Limitations
<!-- Column 1 -->
### 🧭 For a **Content Strategist Persona**
- [ ] Occasionally repetitive with phrasing or keyword use
- [ ] May overanalyze data before taking action
- [ ] Needs clearer boundaries for project scope
- [ ] Can become too focused on SEO metrics over creativity

---
### ✍️ For a **Creative Writing Coach**
- [ ] May over-edit and slow creative flow
- [ ] Tends to focus too much on structure instead of story emotion
- [ ] Occasionally gives abstract feedback
- [ ] Can lose patience with unclear writing goals

<!-- Column 2 -->
### 💡 For a **Technical Mentor Persona**
- [ ] Sometimes gives overly detailed explanations
- [ ] May underestimate beginner struggles
- [ ] Can be rigid with “best practices”
- [ ] Needs to simplify technical language for non-coders
- [ ] 

---
🧪 For a **UX Research Assistant**
- [ ] May rely too heavily on data instead of intuition
- [ ] Needs more empathy in user analysis
- [ ] Can overlook creative design opportunities
- [ ] Occasionally overcomplicates reporting
